Welcome to My Top Hometown Restaurants!

Here, we'll see a list of my favorite restaurants in (or near) my hometown of Hubbard, OH.1

Hubbard, OH is a small town located on the border of Ohio and Pennsylvania. This small town has a population of roughly 7,500 people, which is drastically different from the 86,500 people located in Carmel, IN. Though small, this town provides several opportunities to enjoy local foods and entertainment not usually found in larger cities.

  • Pizza Works
  • Brewtus Brewery
  • Quaker Steak and Lube
  • Katie's Korner Homemade Ice Cream
